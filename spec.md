# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Build a comprehensive insurance management application for KRISHNA TECH AND SOLUTIONS to manage customers, policies, documents, and track expiration dates with alerts.

**Planned changes:**
- Create customer management system with add, view, search, and filter capabilities
- Implement policy management with policy number, type, company, customer assignment, start/expiry dates, and road tax tracking
- Add file upload functionality for policy documents
- Build alerts and notifications system for policy expirations, insurance renewals, and road tax expiry
- Create dashboard showing upcoming renewals and expirations with summary metrics
- Implement navigation menu with Dashboard, Customers, Policies, Alerts, Help, Support, and Legal sections
- Add quick access "Add Policy" option and filter policies by insurance company
- Generate PDF reports for customer data and policy details with export functionality
- Apply company branding: "KRISHNA TECH AND SOLUTIONS" with tagline "IRDAI Registered Insurance Partner | Insurance | CA | Legal" and contact info "6290932283, krishnatechandsolutions@gmail.com"
- Design professional insurance business interface with clear navigation, data tables, and visual alert indicators

**User-visible outcome:** Users can manage customers and insurance policies, upload policy documents, receive visual alerts for upcoming expirations (insurance and road tax), view a dashboard of upcoming renewals, and export customer/policy data as PDF reports. The application features professional branding with KRISHNA TECH AND SOLUTIONS company identity throughout.
